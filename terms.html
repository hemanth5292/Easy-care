Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Easy Care. Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before using our extended warranty services:

1. Eligibility

Only products registered under our Easy Care program during installation or pre-installation are eligible. The program is valid only for products purchased from authorized retailers.

2. Coverage Duration

Coverage options are available from 1 year to 5 years beyond the manufacturer’s warranty period. The duration of coverage is based on the selected plan at the time of purchase.

3. Exclusions

The following are excluded from coverage:

  • Physical or accidental damage caused by negligence or external factors.
  • Unauthorized modifications, repairs, or servicing.
  • Consumable parts such as filters, batteries, and accessories.
  • Products used for commercial or industrial purposes unless explicitly covered.
  • Cosmetic damages that do not affect product functionality.

4. Claims Process

To file a claim, follow these steps:

  • Contact Easy Care customer support with product details, purchase invoice, and warranty registration proof.
  • Schedule a service visit by an authorized technician.
  • Repair or replacement will be carried out based on the technician’s assessment.

Note: The total claim amount across the warranty period cannot exceed the product’s invoice value.

5. Service Limitations

The warranty covers only issues related to manufacturing defects or functional problems. Cosmetic damages are excluded unless they directly affect product functionality.
