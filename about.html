About Us

We specialize in offering Easy Care extended warranty services for newly purchased consumer durables. Easy Care ensures your peace of mind by providing free service and repair activities after the manufacturer’s warranty expires. Our program is designed to deliver reliable support and protection for your valuable purchases, covering up to the product’s invoice value.

Our mission is to provide top-notch warranty services that exceed customer expectations. With Easy Care, you can rest assured that your valuable purchases are in good hands. We have a nationwide network of authorized service centers and a dedicated customer support team ready to assist you with any issues you may encounter.
