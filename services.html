Our Services

Extended Warranty Program

Our extended warranty program provides comprehensive coverage for your consumer durables beyond the manufacturer’s warranty period. Here are some of the key features:

  • Coverage starts immediately after the manufacturer’s warranty ends.
  • Includes free service and repairs, covering labor and parts.
  • Provides access to a nationwide network of authorized service centers.
  • Offers a seamless claims process that is both hassle-free and transparent.
  • The warranty is transferable in case of resale.
  • Customizable plans are available, ranging from 1 to 5 years of coverage.

Eligible Products

Easy Care covers a wide range of consumer durables, including:

  • Home Appliances: Refrigerators, Washing Machines, Dishwashers, Air Conditioners, etc.
  • Consumer Electronics: LED/LCD Televisions, Audio Systems, Laptops, etc.
  • Small Appliances: Microwave Ovens, Vacuum Cleaners, Water Purifiers, etc.
