How Easy Care Works

Our Easy Care Extended Warranty Program is designed to seamlessly extend the protection of your valuable purchases beyond the manufacturer’s warranty period. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how it works:

1. Register Your Product 📋

Ensure your product is enrolled in the Easy Care program at the time of purchase or installation. This can usually be done through the retailer or directly with us.

2. Enjoy Manufacturer Warranty Period 🛠️

During the initial period covered by the manufacturer’s warranty, you can enjoy the original coverage provided by the brand. This includes repairs and services as specified by the manufacturer.

3. Seamless Transition to Easy Care 🔄

Once the manufacturer’s warranty expires, our Easy Care coverage begins automatically. There’s no gap in protection, ensuring continuous peace of mind.

4. Effortless Claims Assistance 🤝

In case of any issues with your product after the manufacturer’s warranty period has ended, simply contact Easy Care customer support. Our team will assist you in filing a claim, scheduling a service visit, and ensuring your product is repaired or replaced as needed.

5. Nationwide Network of Authorized Service Centers 🌐

Easy Care provides access to a nationwide network of authorized service centers, ensuring your product is handled by trained professionals using genuine parts.

6. Transparent and Hassle-Free Process 📝

We pride ourselves on our transparent and hassle-free claims process. All you need to do is provide the necessary details, and our team will take care of the rest.
